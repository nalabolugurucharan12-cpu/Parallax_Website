Endless sky, endless dreams.

SKY

The sky spreads wide above us.

It changes colors with time and mood.

It holds clouds, stars, and dreams.

It reminds us how small worries are.

The earth feels honest.

LAND

The land supports every life.

It grows food and gives shelter.

It carries footsteps and stories

It connects us to our roots..

Strength grows in silence.

MOUNTAINS

Mountains stand strong and silent.

They face storms without moving.

They teach patience and courage.

Their height inspires inner strength.

Calm water, clear mind.

WATER

Water flows without force.

It adapts to every path.

It gives life and calmness.

It teaches us to keep moving.
